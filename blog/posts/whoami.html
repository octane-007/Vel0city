Whoami — Why VoidStack Exists

Posted: June 22, 2025

I’m 0ctane, and I started this platform to help people like me who didn’t know where to begin.

VoidStack is a growing collection of:

  • Free and open-source resources
  • Writeups, walkthroughs, and tool guides
  • Self-hostable labs for hands-on hacking

No ads. No paywalls. Just raw, useful knowledge.

This isn’t a company — it’s a movement. Welcome to the stack.
