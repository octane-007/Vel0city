About VoidStack

VoidStack curates high-quality, 100% free resources across cybersecurity, programming, and digital forensics.

We collect and organize tools, labs, and guides from the internet. Some links may offer optional paid extras — but our core mission remains free and open.

This is a student-led hobby project, not a business. Built using GitHub Pages, with zero hosting or ad cost. Our only cost is the domain and maybe lab hosting — all self-funded.
